Welcome to the TikTok All Favorite Videos Remover. Before getting started, please make sure you are logged in to your TikTok account and on your profile page.

Warning: Keep the tab open until the process is done. Even if in the background or minimized. If you close the tab, the process will stop. If you have a lot of favorite videos, it may take a while. Be patient. You can check the progress in the console.
